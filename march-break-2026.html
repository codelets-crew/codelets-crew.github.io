MAKER QUEST CAMP

march 16-20, 2026

an epic, multi-day stem adventure - from cardboard engineering to 3d printing, robots, coding and giant collaborative builds, this camp delivers five full days of creativity, teamwork and pure maker magic. (AGES 5-12)

Monday: build + imagine plan and construct a life-size cardboard kingdom using our specialized tools (makedo + chompshop).
Tuesday: design + create learn the basics of 3d design in tinkercad and create your own custom badge or nameplate, then watch it come to life on our 3d printers!
Wednesday: connect + collaborate work together to build mega-sized mazes, floor triggers and giant gamepads using makey makey technology.
Thursday: construct + explore learn how sensors, motors and logic blocks work while you race, test and tune your bot through quests and challenges.
Friday: invent + showcase create simple machines powered by micro:bit and makecode. bring them to life and show them off at pickup time!
Location: THE FORGE | 77 Light st, Woodstock, ON
Dates: Monday, March 16 - Friday, March 20
Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
(before & after care available | $8/day)
Outdoor Activities: enjoy organized games, sports & activities at nearby Victoria park and Central Public School every day!
Field Trip: after lunch on Wednesday, spend an afternoon running, climbing and sliding at Jax Jungle. There is an additional fee of $5/child for this trip, we've subsidized the rest of the cost!
