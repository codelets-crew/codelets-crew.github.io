Playground

hands-on stem mash-up for curious 4-6 year olds

a play-based program to ignite and foster a lifelong love of all things coding, robotics and making

The Playground also builds the foundational thinking skills that set children up for future success in the Core and Studio programs.

Coding: Level One

Arcade Creators

Dive into the world of game design as you learn the fundamentals of coding through building your very own playable arcade game. We’ll explore pixel art, sprite animation, character logic, scoring systems and level design while learning how to prototype, test and debug like real developers. Studio

a building & tinkering makerspace

monthly themed maker challenges, where kids are free to direct their own journey of discovery - with full mentor support

The studio is an open makerspace where creativity and independence thrive. Each month features a new theme, with suggested projects to explore - or the freedom to design your own. Our instructors offer mentorship and support throughout, helping kids problem-solve confidently without feeling stuck.

January: Winter Wonderland

Example Projects:

  • Build a snow shoveling robot
  • Code a snowman runner game
  • 3D Design & print snowflake jewelry
  • DIY a winter photobooth using cardboard, makedo tools and Adobe Express/Canva
