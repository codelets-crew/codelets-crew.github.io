The Build Lab

PA Day Camp

a full day of bricks, blocks and imagination!

Get ready to spend the day imagining, designing, prototyping, building and rebuilding with blocks, bricks, tiles and carboard. Each camper will build independently and collaboratively throughout the day - creating cityscapes, reimagining natural structures and maybe even bringing some nether fortresses to life!

We'll break up the day with a pizza lunch, followed by some organized outdoor fun at the Central Public School play yard.

Location: THE FORGE | 77 Light St in Woodstock
Date: Friday, January 30th
Hours: 9am-4pm (extended hours available from 8am to 5pm)
Ages: 5-12
Cost: $70
